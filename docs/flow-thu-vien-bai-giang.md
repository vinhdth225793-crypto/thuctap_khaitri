# FLOW HE THONG MOI CHO THU VIEN, BAI GIANG VA TAI LIEU

## 1. Muc tieu tai lieu

Tai lieu nay mo ta flow nghiep vu moi cua he thong sau khi bo sung chuc nang `Thu vien`.

Muc tieu:
- tach ro noi `chuan bi tai nguyen` va noi `dua tai nguyen vao khoa hoc`
- toi uu truong hop video can xu ly truoc khi dua vao bai giang
- ho tro nhieu loai tai lieu nhu video, PDF, PowerPoint, Word, audio, anh, file nen, link ngoai
- giup giang vien co the soan bai giang nhanh hon, khong phai cho xu ly video trong luc dang chinh sua khoa hoc

---

## 2. Khai niem chinh

### 2.1. Thu vien

`Thu vien` la kho luu tru tai nguyen dung chung de giang vien chuan bi truoc.

Tai nguyen trong Thu vien:
- chua gan ngay vao mot buoi hoc cu the
- co the duoc tai len, xu ly, luu tru va tai su dung nhieu lan
- co the duoc chon lai khi tao bai giang trong khoa hoc

### 2.2. Bai giang

`Bai giang` la noi dung xuat hien ben trong khoa hoc, module, buoi hoc.

Moi bai giang:
- thuoc ve dung 1 khoa hoc
- thuoc ve dung 1 module
- thuoc ve dung 1 buoi hoc
- co tieu de ro rang
- co loai bai giang
- co the lien ket toi 1 tai nguyen trong Thu vien

### 2.3. Tai lieu

`Tai lieu` la tep hoac lien ket duoc luu trong Thu vien de sau do dua vao bai giang.

### 2.4. Noi dung khoa hoc

Noi dung khoa hoc di theo cau truc:

`Khoa hoc -> Module -> Buoi hoc -> Bai giang`

Thu vien nam ben ngoai flow nay, dong vai tro la kho tai nguyen dau vao.

---

## 3. Vai tro lien quan

### 3.1. Admin
- tao khoa hoc, module, buoi hoc
- quan ly cau truc noi dung tong the neu can
- co the xem va ho tro quan tri tai nguyen neu he thong cho phep

### 3.2. Giang vien
- tai tai nguyen len Thu vien
- theo doi trang thai xu ly cua video
- vao khoa hoc de tao va sua bai giang
- chon tai nguyen tu Thu vien de dua vao bai giang

### 3.3. Hoc vien
- xem bai giang va tai lieu sau khi duoc dua vao khoa hoc va du dieu kien hien thi

---

## 4. Danh sach loai tai lieu khi them vao Thu vien

Tai man hinh `Them tai lieu vao Thu vien`, can co truong `Loai tai lieu`.

De xuat danh sach chon:
- `video`
- `pdf`
- `powerpoint`
- `word`
- `excel`
- `audio`
- `image`
- `archive`
- `link_ngoai`
- `tai_lieu_khac`

Y nghia:
- `video`: can di qua buoc xu ly video
- `pdf`, `powerpoint`, `word`, `excel`, `audio`, `image`, `archive`: luu file vao Thu vien
- `link_ngoai`: khong upload file, chi luu lien ket
- `tai_lieu_khac`: dung cho cac dinh dang chua duoc tach rieng

---

## 5. Trang thai cua tai nguyen trong Thu vien

### 5.1. Trang thai xu ly

De xuat flow trang thai xu ly:
- `nhap`
- `dang_tai_len`
- `dang_xu_ly`
- `san_sang`
- `loi_xu_ly`

### 5.2. Y nghia

- `nhap`: moi tao ban ghi, chua co du lieu hoac chua xac nhan
- `dang_tai_len`: he thong dang nhan file
- `dang_xu_ly`: ap dung cho video can ma hoa, toi uu, bao mat
- `san_sang`: co the chon de dua vao bai giang
- `loi_xu_ly`: video hoac file co van de, chua duoc dung

Luu y:
- voi file thuong nhu PDF hoac Word, he thong co the di thang sang `san_sang`
- voi video, he thong nen bat buoc di qua `dang_xu_ly`

---

## 6. Flow tong the cua he thong moi

### Flow tong quan

1. Giang vien vao `Thu vien`.
2. Giang vien chon `Loai tai lieu` va tai tep hoac nhap link.
3. He thong luu tai nguyen vao Thu vien.
4. Neu la video, he thong xu ly video truoc.
5. Khi tai nguyen dat trang thai `san_sang`, giang vien vao khoa hoc can sua.
6. Giang vien vao tab `Noi dung`, chon module, buoi hoc.
7. Giang vien tao bai giang moi hoac sua bai giang da co.
8. Giang vien chon `Loai bai giang`.
9. Neu bai giang can tai nguyen, he thong mo bo chon tai nguyen tu Thu vien.
10. Giang vien chon tai nguyen da `san_sang`.
11. He thong lien ket tai nguyen Thu vien vao bai giang cua buoi hoc.
12. Khoa hoc duoc hoan thien va hoc vien se xem bai giang theo quyen hien thi.

---

## 7. Flow chi tiet theo nhu cau moi

## 7.1. Buoc 1: Chuan bi va xu ly video tai Thu vien

### Muc tieu

Tai video len truoc de he thong xu ly san, tranh viec giang vien phai cho trong luc soan bai giang.

### Hanh dong

1. Giang vien vao menu `Thu vien`.
2. Bam `Them tai lieu`.
3. Chon `Loai tai lieu = video`.
4. Nhap thong tin co ban:
   - tieu de tai lieu
   - mo ta ngan
   - tu khoa neu can
   - tep video
5. He thong luu ban ghi Thu vien.
6. He thong dua video vao hang doi xu ly.
7. He thong ma hoa, toi uu chat luong, tao dinh dang phat video va ap dung co che bao mat.
8. Khi xu ly xong, trang thai chuyen sang `san_sang`.

### Ket qua

- video da duoc xu ly truoc
- giang vien co the dung lai video nay cho 1 hoac nhieu bai giang
- khi vao khoa hoc, giang vien khong phai cho he thong xu ly nua

### Ghi chu nghiep vu

- nen khuyen khich giang vien tai toan bo video len Thu vien truoc khi soan khoa hoc
- video chua `san_sang` thi khong duoc chon vao bai giang

---

## 7.2. Buoc 2: Truy cap va soan thao bai giang trong khoa hoc

### Muc tieu

Di chuyen den dung khoa hoc, module, buoi hoc de dua tai nguyen vao noi dung giang day.

### Hanh dong

1. Giang vien vao khoa hoc can chinh sua.
2. Neu chua co khoa hoc, admin hoac nguoi co quyen tao khoa hoc moi.
3. Tai tab `Noi dung`, giang vien thay cau truc:
   - khoa hoc
   - module
   - buoi hoc
   - bai giang
4. Giang vien chon:
   - `Tao bai giang moi`
   - hoac `Sua bai giang da co`
5. Giang vien nhap thong tin bai giang:
   - tieu de bai giang
   - mo ta ngan neu can
   - loai bai giang
   - thu tu hien thi
6. Giang vien luu bai giang vao buoi hoc tuong ung.

### Ket qua

- bai giang ton tai trong dung khoa hoc, module, buoi hoc
- bai giang da san sang de gan tai nguyen tu Thu vien

### Ghi chu nghiep vu

- bai giang la don vi xuat hien trong noi dung khoa hoc
- Thu vien chi la noi chuan bi tai nguyen, khong thay the bai giang

---

## 7.3. Buoc 3: Them video vao bai giang

### Muc tieu

Gan video da xu ly xong trong Thu vien vao bai giang cua buoi hoc.

### Hanh dong

1. Tai giao dien sua bai giang, giang vien chon `Loai bai giang = Video`.
2. Nhap `Tieu de bai giang`.
3. He thong hien `Nguon video`.
4. Giang vien chon `Chon tu Thu vien`.
5. He thong mo danh sach tai nguyen Thu vien da loc:
   - loai tai lieu = video
   - trang thai = san_sang
6. Giang vien chon 1 video can dung.
7. He thong gan `tai_nguyen_thu_vien_id` vao bai giang.
8. Giang vien luu bai giang.

### Ket qua

- bai giang video da nam trong khoa hoc
- video phat duoc ngay khi bai giang duoc mo
- khong phai xu ly video lai trong luc soan bai giang

---

## 7.4. Flow cho cac loai tai lieu khac

Ngoai video, bai giang co the gan cac tai nguyen khac tu Thu vien.

### Truong hop PDF

1. Giang vien vao Thu vien.
2. Chon `Loai tai lieu = pdf`.
3. Tai file len.
4. He thong luu va dua trang thai `san_sang`.
5. Trong bai giang, giang vien chon loai bai giang phu hop:
   - `Tai lieu PDF`
   - hoac `Tai lieu dinh kem`
6. Chon file PDF tu Thu vien.

### Truong hop PowerPoint / Word / Excel

1. Giang vien tai file len Thu vien.
2. He thong luu file va danh dau `san_sang`.
3. Trong bai giang, giang vien chon tai nguyen tu Thu vien de gan vao buoi hoc.

### Truong hop link ngoai

1. Giang vien vao Thu vien.
2. Chon `Loai tai lieu = link_ngoai`.
3. Nhap URL, tieu de, mo ta.
4. He thong luu ban ghi ma khong can upload file.
5. Trong bai giang, giang vien chon lien ket nay de dua vao noi dung.

---

## 8. Nguyen tac phan biet giua Thu vien va Bai giang

### Thu vien dung de
- tai file len truoc
- xu ly video
- luu tai nguyen dung chung
- tai su dung tai nguyen cho nhieu bai giang

### Bai giang dung de
- xep noi dung vao khoa hoc
- gan tai nguyen vao dung module, buoi hoc
- hien thi cho hoc vien theo trinh tu hoc tap

Ket luan:
- `Thu vien = kho tai nguyen`
- `Bai giang = noi dung hoc tap trong khoa hoc`

---

## 9. Flow hien thi cho hoc vien

Flow hoc vien sau khi he thong moi duoc ap dung:

1. Hoc vien vao khoa hoc.
2. Hoc vien vao module.
3. Hoc vien vao buoi hoc.
4. Hoc vien thay danh sach bai giang cua buoi hoc.
5. Moi bai giang hien:
   - tieu de
   - loai bai giang
   - video hoac tai lieu lien ket
6. Neu bai giang la video, hoc vien xem video da duoc xu ly san.
7. Neu bai giang la PDF hoac file tai lieu, hoc vien xem hoac tai tep theo quyen.

Luu y:
- hoc vien khong truy cap truc tiep vao Thu vien
- hoc vien chi truy cap bai giang da duoc dua vao khoa hoc

---

## 10. Quy tac nghiep vu de xuat

### 10.1. Quy tac cho video
- video phai duoc dua vao Thu vien truoc
- chi video `san_sang` moi duoc dua vao bai giang

### 10.2. Quy tac cho bai giang
- moi bai giang phai thuoc mot buoi hoc cu the
- moi bai giang phai co tieu de
- bai giang co `loai bai giang`

### 10.3. Quy tac cho tai nguyen Thu vien
- moi tai nguyen phai co `loai tai lieu`
- moi tai nguyen co the duoc tai su dung
- tai nguyen video va tai nguyen file can luu metadata de phuc vu quan ly

### 10.4. Quy tac hien thi
- tai nguyen trong Thu vien khong tu dong hien cho hoc vien
- chi khi duoc gan vao bai giang thi hoc vien moi thay

---

## 11. De xuat UI toi thieu

### 11.1. Man hinh Thu vien
- danh sach tai nguyen
- bo loc theo loai tai lieu
- bo loc theo trang thai xu ly
- nut `Them tai lieu`
- cot:
  - tieu de
  - loai tai lieu
  - trang thai
  - ngay tao
  - thao tac

### 11.2. Form them tai lieu vao Thu vien
- loai tai lieu
- tieu de
- mo ta
- file upload hoac link ngoai
- thumbnail neu can

### 11.3. Man hinh sua bai giang
- tieu de bai giang
- loai bai giang
- nut `Chon tu Thu vien`
- danh sach tai nguyen da chon

---

## 12. Flow he thong duoc chot de code tiep

Day la flow nghiep vu de xay dung trong cac buoc tiep theo:

1. Giang vien tai tai nguyen len `Thu vien`.
2. He thong xu ly video truoc.
3. Giang vien vao `Khoa hoc -> Noi dung -> Module -> Buoi hoc`.
4. Giang vien tao hoac sua `Bai giang`.
5. Giang vien chon `Loai bai giang`.
6. Giang vien gan tai nguyen tu `Thu vien`.
7. He thong luu lien ket giua `Bai giang` va `Tai nguyen Thu vien`.
8. Hoc vien hoc theo bai giang trong khoa hoc, khong di truc tiep vao Thu vien.

---

## 13. Ket luan

Flow moi giai quyet dung van de ma ban dat ra:
- co `Thu vien` de chuan bi va luu bai giang, tai lieu
- co buoc xu ly video truoc khi dua vao khoa hoc
- co flow soan thao bai giang trong khoa hoc ro rang
- co bo chon `Loai tai lieu` khi them vao Thu vien
- ho tro nhieu loai tai lieu nhu video, PDF va cac file khac

Tai lieu nay se la nen de sua tiep:
- schema
- model
- route
- controller
- giao dien Thu vien
- giao dien bai giang trong khoa hoc
